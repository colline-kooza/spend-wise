## About Desishub

**Desishub Technologies Co. Ltd** is a registered technology solutions company based in Kampala, Uganda. Founded in 2022 as a business venture and officially registered as a company in 2025, Desishub has rapidly grown into a full-service technology partner specializing in custom software development, mobile applications, and digital solutions.

### Our Mission

To deliver innovative, scalable, and user-centric technology solutions that solve real-world business challenges while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer satisfaction.

### Company Information

- **Registered Name:** Desishub Technologies Co. Ltd
- **Founded:** 2022 (Registered as Company: 2025)
- **Location:** Kireka, Kampala City, Uganda
- **Team Size:** 18 dedicated professionals across 5 specialized teams
- **Leadership:** Muke Johnbaptist (JB) - Founder & Technical Lead

## Our Services

### 1. Custom Web Development

Our core strength lies in building bespoke management systems tailored to solve specific business challenges. We partner with companies and NGOs to create powerful, scalable solutions.

**Featured Systems:**

- **Limibooks** - Comprehensive Inventory and Accounting Management System
- **Lendbox** - Advanced Loan Management System
- **Lectify** - Complete School Management System
- **TrackIt** - Shipment and Logistics Management System

We also design and develop professional company websites, NGO websites, and custom web portals that reflect your brand identity and drive business growth.

### 2. Custom Mobile Application Development

Similar to our web development capabilities, we create custom mobile applications for bespoke business systems. Our mobile solutions are designed for performance, usability, and seamless integration with existing systems.

## Our Technology Stack

We believe in using the right technology for each project, with our stack selection often guided by project scope, scale, and client budget.

### For Custom Websites

- **Next.js** + **Tailwind CSS** + **React** + **TypeScript**

### For Small-Scale Systems

- **Next.js Fullstack** (ideal for projects with few users and budget constraints)

### Standard Stack (Small to Medium Projects)

- **Frontend:** Next.js
- **Backend API:** Hono
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **ORM:** Prisma

### Enterprise Stack (Medium to Large-Scale Applications)

Our upgraded stack for high-ticket clients and enterprise-level applications:

- **Frontend:** Next.js
- **Backend API:** Go (GIN Framework)
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **ORM:** GORM

### Flexible Backend Solutions

- **Laravel** - For specific client requirements
- **Node.js** - For specialized projects

### Infrastructure & Tools

- **File Storage:** AWS S3, Cloudflare R2, Uploadthing
- **Hosting:** Vercel, Digital Ocean, Dockploy
